Common Help

How do I start realtime audio translation?

Open SonaCap, choose input and output languages, tap the Broadcast button, select SonaCap Capture, and start Broadcast. Then play audio in the built-in browser or another app while SonaCap remains open.

Why do captions sometimes lag or miss words?

Speech recognition and translation quality can be affected by audio quality, background noise, language choice, device performance, iOS Broadcast behavior, Apple Speech, Apple Translation, and local fallback models.

Is speech recognition local?

By default, SonaCap requires Apple Speech to process speech recognition on device when the selected input language supports it. You can change Local Apple Speech in the app's About menu.

Does webpage text translation count toward the audio trial?

No. Webpage text translation is separate from realtime audio subtitle translation. Realtime audio subtitles include 5 cumulative free minutes before Full Unlock is required.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open SonaCap, go to About > Purchase & Restore, and use Restore Purchase.

Important Safety Notice

Captions may update quickly. Stop using the app immediately if rapid text changes, subtitles, video, or screen motion cause dizziness, eye strain, nausea, headache, discomfort, or any other adverse symptoms.

Do not rely on SonaCap for emergency, medical, legal, safety-critical, financial, navigation, or other high-stakes decisions.
