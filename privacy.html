Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Overview

SonaCap is designed to provide realtime captions and translation while storing as little user information as possible.

Audio, Speech Recognition, and Translation

SonaCap may use iOS Broadcast, Apple Speech, Apple Translation, and local fallback speech recognition models to provide captions and translations.

Speech recognition and translation behavior may depend on Apple system services and on-device models. Review Apple's privacy information for details about Apple services.

Information Stored On Device

SonaCap may store language preferences, bookmarks, first-launch disclaimer acceptance, trial counters, and purchase entitlement state on your device.

Diagnostics

Diagnostic tools and temporary debug audio files are intended for development or diagnostic builds only and should not be included in the App Store release build.

Third-Party Content

SonaCap can be used with third-party apps, websites, audio, and videos. SonaCap does not control those third-party services or their privacy practices.

Contact

For privacy questions, email sonacap.support@gmail.com.
